About

Over the years, I have specialized in developing innovative, functional, and scalable digital experiences with a strong focus on design fidelity, responsiveness, and performance optimization.

With expertise in HTML, CSS (Sass, Tailwind), JavaScript (Vanilla, ReactJS, NextJS), and PHP, I have built custom web solutions using CMS platforms like WordPress, CraftCMS, and Strapi. I am dedicated to delivering user-centric applications that align with modern web standards, including accessibility and SEO best practices.

I have hands-on experience in creating high-traffic websites, ensuring clean and maintainable codebases for long-term scalability. My background includes transforming Figma designs into pixel-perfect implementations and leveraging tools like Docker for consistent workflows and deployment.

I hold a Bachelor of Computer Science and have completed certifications in AWS, full-stack development, Git/GitHub and many more.

Fluent in Portuguese and English, I have a history of excelling in collaborative, dynamic, and multicultural environments.
